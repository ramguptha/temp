Setting the passcode on your Android device

You can issue a command to remotely set a new passcode on your Android device.

After you issue the command, the passcode on your Android device is set as soon as it next contacts the notification server of its OS vendor. If the device is connected to a Wi-Fi or mobile network, this action is usually carried out within a minute; otherwise, it happens when the device reconnects to a network.

To set a new passcode on your Android device:

1. In the My Devices sidebar, select the Android device whose passcode you want to set.
2. From the Commands menu, click Set Passcode.
3. Enter and confirm the new passcode in the fields provided.
4. Click Set Passcode.
5. Click OK to close the confirmation message.
